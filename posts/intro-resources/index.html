Resources for Prospective Politics Students

If you are thinking about studying Politics or want to know more about what the subject might look like, the following material can provide you an accessible introduction, in different media, to some of themes and ways in the social and political sciences:

Under construction

Books

  • Daron Acemoglu and James Robinson. Why nations fail. 2012.
  • Francis Fukuyama. The Origins of Political Order: From Prehuman Times to the French Revolution. 2011.
  • Bernard Crick. Democracy: A very short introduction. 2002.
  • Kenneth Minogue. Politics: A very short introduction. 2000.
  • Cas Mudde and Cristóbal Rovira Kaltwasser. Populism: A very short introduction. 2017.
  • David Miller. Political philosophy: A very short introduction. 2003.
